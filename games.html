PROJECT 1

Dead Apocalypse

Inspired by the text-based adventure games of olde, my client Madsox Games wanted to make a zombie apocalypse adventure with story-driven gameplay, a simple UI system, and visceral comic-book style graphics.

Will you survive, or die?



Explore unique environments

My Role

As the primary creative member on the team I generated all visual-based assets from placeholder examples. I provided creative direction for gameplay, UI/UX, environment design, and marketing materials.

What I did

  • Mapped out the user-flow.
  • Created UI Assets.
  • Designated UX requirements.
  • Created static and animated marketing and environment artwork.

PROJECT 2

Go Go Penguin

One of my more interesting projects, Go Go Penguin (working title) was an in-progress physics game was meant to teach players about nature in the arctic. Unfortunately due to an emergency at the north pole my client was unable to complete the project.

What I did

  • Worked with the client to establish a visual style.
  • Created infinitely tiling textures for foreground assets.
  • Created tiling parallax layer models for background assets.
  • Generated UI Concepts (unable to share).


Inifinitely tiling textures helped cut down on asset costs

Parallax backgrounds tiled as well, an important factor to establish atmosphere.
