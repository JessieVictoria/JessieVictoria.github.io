FUNDAMENTALS OF DESIGN

What is it I do exactly?

Research

Review benchmark products and user needs, use this information to inform the broad strokes of the project.

Concept

Generate a series of low fidelity "box" wireframes or bullet-point concepts to discuss with stakeholders.

Deliverables

Create wireframes, annotations, and prototypes to get the concept nailed down, tested, and easily iterated.

Testing

Review the implemented design against what was found in the research phase, modify as needed.
