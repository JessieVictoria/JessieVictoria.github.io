InstaRyde

InstaRyde approached my team for a ride-share application development, eventually leading to us being absorbed into the InstaRyde team as founders. I was the UI/UX Design Lead working under the creative director to ensure brand cohesion.

The Problem

Toronto didn't have a ride-share solution at the time, and our team strived to become that solution.

My Role

UI/UX Design Lead, Asset Creation, Design System Owner, Wireframing and Prototyping, Branding Design, Testing Against Design Systems, Customer Experience Design

InstaRyde Canada, Toronto, ON

2014 - 2016

Benchmark Research

When we began this project we (my team at Hak Studio) were hired as branding specialists, our client wanted a friendlier brand than the leading ride-share companies at the time. Looking at the existing marketplace, and brands which matched our client goals, we established brand and market guidelines with colours and images which matched the target consumers. We built each system from the ground up, keeping customer experience at the core of our efforts.
