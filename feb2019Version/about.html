Hello.

I’m a well rounded interaction designer with a focus on visual and experience design for digital products. My designs are a collaboration of creative problem solving and user-centered design principles.

Coming from an illustration degree and creative background I’ve always been fascinated with the world of visual communication, and getting users/viewers from point A to point B effortlessly. I’ve worked with a variety of products and teams, from early-stage startups to enterprise products and AAA games.

I am passionate about a lot of things, when I’m not working on something awesome you can find me playing video games, hanging out with my pet chinchillas, or exploring the world through camping and hiking.

Once you get to know me you will find I am eager to learn about anything that crosses my path. I often get into related disciplines like learning to code or game-design principles, to craftier skills like macrame and knitting, I am here to learn.
