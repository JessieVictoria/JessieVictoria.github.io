Pogo Games Deliverables

My work with Pogo Games, a division of Electronic Arts.

The Multiplayer System

Pogo needed some wireframes to get the discussion started around their upcoming multiplayer system redesign.

Settings

Horizontal bars were the concept here, allowing users to focus on one action per row.



Searching

Wires were created to dictate the layout and look of the search flow for users who may not be familiar with standard search experiences.

Canasta

Canasta is a complex card game which involves remembering a lot of contextual rules to play. Our primary goal with this project was to put all the information on the screen as needed to help onboarding and mastery.



Canasta Layouts

The goal here was to add the required information to make the first few moves, surface the relevant information in a visual way, and improve the scoring menu for improved comprehension of the points system.
